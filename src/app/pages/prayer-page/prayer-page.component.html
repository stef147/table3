Prayer Room

“My house will be called a house of prayer” – Jesus (Matt 21:13)

“Apart from me you can do nothing” – Jesus (John 15:5)

We prioritise prayer and firmly believe that nothing will be achieved without seeking to know and be aligned with what God wants to do. The Prayer Room at the Table Hub is open for at least an hour every day for people to intercede on behalf of this community, town and nation.

We have corporate prayer on Tuesday nights from 7:45-9pm
